TEAM STORIES

Gear Clash is the largest high school robotics battling event that involves international teams
competiting and learning from each other.The event develops important STEAM skills such as
building a robot from scratch, writing codes in C++ and following the design process.

Every year, we have more than 50 countries and 200 teams participating and we welcome
more enthusiastic young engineers to join us on this incredible journey!
